Founded in 1922, Frost Electric is a family owned and operated business in Aurora.

Our electricians are International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers members, and the business is a member of the National Electrical Contractors Association. We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial work. We also do lighting and building electrical maintenance, as well as design build work.
